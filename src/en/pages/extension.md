# Domain Bookmarks & Site Notes Extension {:.text-4xl .font-bold .text-gray-800 .mb-4}

**Tame browser clutter and upgrade your browsing efficiency.** While bookmarklets are perfect for instant client-side tasks, keeping dozens of them on your browser bookmark bar quickly exhausts your screen space.

The **Domain Bookmarks & Site Notes Extension** is the ultimate power-tool designed to solve browser clutter. It consolidates all your favorite scripts, bookmarks, page notes, and web reminders into one beautiful, search-activated control panel.

---

## ⚡ Main Features {:.text-2xl .font-bold .text-gray-800 .mt-8 .mb-4}

### 🔍 1. Domain-Aware Bookmarks
* Pop open the extension, and it instantly filters and displays all bookmarklets and bookmarks saved for the current site you are viewing.
* Global search lets you find any bookmark or script across folders in a split-second, without ever searching through nested dropdown menus.

### 📝 2. Page-level Sticky Notes
* Right-click anywhere on a webpage to attach a contextual note exactly where you clicked.
* Notes support full Markdown syntax, auto-saving, color-coding, resizing, and pinning so they remain visible when you revisit the site.

### ⏰ 3. Bookmark Reminders
* Never forget to revisit saved articles, documentation, or links. Set one-time or recurring revisit monitors.
* Features in-tab unobtrusive toast notifications while you're active, with desktop system notifications as a backup.

### 🧹 4. Smart Links Cleanup
* Built-in browser utilities to instantly scan your bookmark files:
  * Detect and prune broken/dead links.
  * Delete duplicate bookmarks in one click.
  * Clean up empty folders.

### 📊 5. Activity Dashboard
* Track your bookmarking patterns, folder expansion velocity, and domain activity breakdowns over time.
