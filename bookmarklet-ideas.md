## 🔍 SEO Keyword Opportunities

Based on research, these search patterns get consistent traffic:

- **`[task] bookmarklet`** — e.g., "accessibility check bookmarklet", "screenshot bookmarklet"
- **`best bookmarklets for [profession]`** — e.g., "best bookmarklets for developers", "for SEO"
- **`browser hacks [year]`** — e.g., "browser hacks 2026"
- **`lightweight alternative to [extension]`** — e.g., "lightweight alternative to Lighthouse extension"
- **`how to [task] without extension`** — e.g., "how to check accessibility without extension"

### Missing Tags Worth Adding to metadata.json

- `ai` — for AI/ChatGPT bookmarklets (huge search volume right now)
- `accessibility` / `a11y` — growing niche with less competition
- `privacy` — trending topic, pairs well with cookie/tracker tools

---

## 🏆 Top 5 Recommendations to Build First

1. **Dark Mode Toggle** — Massive search volume, dead simple to build, appeals to everyone
2. **AI Context Scraper** — Grab page text → send to ChatGPT. Gen Z gold.
3. **Heading Hierarchy Map** — Unique SEO tool, fills a gap in current SEO collection
4. **Reader View** — Universally useful, competes with Firefox's built-in reader
5. **Clear Site Cookies** — Privacy-focused, fills the "privacy" tag gap
